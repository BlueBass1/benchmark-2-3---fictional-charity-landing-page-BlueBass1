Welcome to the Cybernetic Enchancement (CE) charity

A charity ran by "Technos" for the devlopment of Cybernetic enchancements

We here at Technos are have witnessed strides for the future of Cybernetics not only in our own research centers, but also in the inspiring progress made by other scientists! With what we've seen, we felt motivated to aid fellow researchers in their financial endeavours. We have spent over a $2.5 million throughout 5 years for funding promising projects.

As a company, we have been focused on making advancements in the field Cybernetics. Our passion led us to realize that we could help create progress beyond our own organization. We knew that we could shift some of our funds to other groups who had potential, but lacked the funds to sustain their projects. After we took action and started to fund what we could to other researchers, we began to acknowledge that we could do even more. So we started the CE charity.

Our goal is to raise even more funding so that we can encourage new life to join the field despite financial obstacles. We also plan to bridge the gap between the researchers and the common people. We have a newsletter that brings updates to those who want to learn more about what goes on in the world of cybernetics. It includes personal stories from interviews we have hosted, and it will display the work that is going towards a better tomorrow.
